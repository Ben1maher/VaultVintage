Our Story

Shop Vault Vintage, curated by Giulia Morabito, specializes in unique vintage handbags. Each piece is kept in pristine condition and is usually a one-off, making them very rare. Our mission is to keep old vintage styles alive and to celebrate the charm and history of vintage fashion.
